Reading 11

1. “Our work focuses on processes rather than products: things that adapt to their environment, emphasize change and show difference”
Process is what separates designers and consumers. Consumers value the end product because that is what’s relevant to them. However, designers must value how a product is created in order for it to be successful and thus be relevant to consumers.

2, Create generative work that can change, permutate, and be reproduced by others.
