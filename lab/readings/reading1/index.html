About Low-Tech Magazine’s Solar Powered Website

In similar ways to fast fashion, websites are becoming short-lived and the consequences of the energy usage and storage are not addressed. The Low-Tech website minimizes the web design, creating a static site, in order to to decrease energy usage which reflects how it isn’t necessary to have decoration and a polished and refined layout to successfully carry out a purpose.

Accessibility According to Actual People with Disabilities

Keep people’s disabilities in mind when creating a website. In order to make a site accessible for a large audience, websites should be designed to make it easier for people to read and navigate the site whether that be selecting certain colors or making the text larger.
